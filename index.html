Dr. Manuel Elkin Patarroyo

Here's a time line of Dr. Patarroyo life:

  • 1946: Born on November 3th in Ataco, Tolima, Colombia
  • 1971: Finished got his degree in medicine and surgery at the National University of Colombia
  • 1972: He founded the Institute of Immunology, legally attached to the Foundation San Juan de Dios (Santafé de Bogotá, Colombia)
  • 1986-1988: Patarroyo created and successfully tested synthetic SPf66 vaccine for preventing and curing malaria or malaria.
  • 1989: He has received some national and foreign decorations, such as the national prize of Sciences "Alejandro Ángel Escobar" of the State of São Paulo - in mode of science and technology.
  • 1993: He donated to humanity the first antidote against malaria
  • 2018: He had been working and experimentation continuesly until present looking for new antidotes against different viral diseases.

Here some distinctions and awards:

  • 1989: El ACAC al mérito científico, otorgado por la Asociación Colombiana para el Avance de la Ciencia.
  • 1994: Premio Príncipe de Asturias de Investigación Científica y Técnica.
  • 1995: El Premio de la Fundación Léon Bernard que otorga la Organización Mundial de la Salud (OMS).
  • 2010: Premio HazTUacción,concedido por Fundación AISGE.
  • 2011: Premio Príncipe de Viana de la Solidaridad.
